Albert Einstein

Revolutionary physicist who reshaped our understanding of the universe with his theory of relativity.

Here's a time line of Albert's life:

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Kingdom of Württemberg, German Empire
  • 1895 - Took the entrance examinations for the Swiss Federal polytechnic school in Zürich (later the Eidgenössische Technische Hochschule, ETH)
  • 1900 - Passed the exams in Maths and Physics and was awarded a Federal teaching diploma.
  • 1905 - Einstein's paper "Folgerungen aus den Capillaritätserscheinungen" ("Conclusions from the Capillarity Phenomena") was published in the journal Annalen der Physik.
  • 1908 - Recognized as a leading scientist and was appointed lecturer at the University of Bern.
  • 1915 - Successfully completed his general theory of relativity, which he used to calculate that deflection, and the perihelion precession of Mercury
  • 1922 - Recieved Nobel Prize for Physics
  • 1955 - He died
